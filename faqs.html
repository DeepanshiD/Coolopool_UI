About CoolOPool

What exactly is MoneyApp?

It's an easy-to-use app aimed to make sending and receiving money fast and safe.

What are the fees involved?

The fees are defined depending on the plan you've chosen, no extra payments needed.

Do I need to link my bank account in order to use it?

Yes, you should create your account using your banking credentials.

Does it cost me anything to receive money?

You won't be charged for transactions with MoneyApp.

Can I send more than one at a time?

Sure, you can realise more payments depending on the pricing plan chosen.
